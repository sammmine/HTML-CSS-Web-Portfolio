Hi! My name is Samantha Laqueenna Ginting, but you can call me Lana. I am an Informatics Engineering student who aspires to integrate her technical skills with creativity. My line of work consists of graphic & 3D designs, UI/UX design, and frontend engineering.

Some of My Best(?) Works:

    • UI/UX Design: Parade Wisuda Oktober ITB 2025
    • On September of 2025, I was trusted by a friend to lead the UI/UX Web Design sub-division for Parade Wisuda Oktober ITB 2025. Parade Wisuda Oktober ITB is a campus event that was held to celebrate the recent graduates. This website was created to showcase theses, photographs, and provide event informations to the graduates and campus community. My job consists of creating interactive website layouts, designing each page, and maintaining the visual quality of the website being developed. This work is in progress, and you should see the finished website really soon!
    • UI/UX Design: Berantas Bersama
    • On August 2025, I passed the selection process for my faculty’s Software Engineering Laboratory assistant. One of the assessment tasks was to create a prototype of the software I had designed. Berantas Bersama is an application aimed to eradicate fake news (hoax) on the internet. Collaborating with the Indonesian Ministry of Communication and Digital (Komdigi), this application is expected to foster a healthy digital democracy in Indonesia. The core features of Berantas Bersama are the search and report feature of fake news and digital forums.
    • Frontend Engineering: Little Alchemy Cookbook
    • On June 2025, two of my friends and I made this website for our Algorithm Strategy class. Little Alchemy Cookbook is a web-based application for searching element recipes in Little Alchemy 2 using BFS and DFS strategies. The frontend of this website is built using the React.js framework, while the backend is built using the Go language. I am in charge of designing the appearance of this website and connecting the search algorithm, data, and interactive displays.
    • Graphic Design: HMIF Lakara 2025/2026
    • Since 2024, I have been a part of Himpunan Mahasiswa Informatika ITB’s (HMIF ITB) Visual and Design division. On June 2025, I was elected to be the manager of the division for the new cabinet, Lakara. I am in charge of making the Grand Design Visual, alongside my deputy manager and directors. Now, with my team, we are not only responsible for designing but also maintaining the quality of HMIF’s visual branding.
    • 3D Design: Rainwater Filter Animation
    • Since 2024, I have been freelancing as a graphic and 3D designer. So far, my clients are all college students who need help for their college assignment models.
